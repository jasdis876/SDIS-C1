Component I – Foundations of Contact Investigation and Disease Intervention in Jamaica

Introduction to Contact Investigation/Disease Intervention in Jamaica, based on the MOHW Contact Investigation Field Guide.

Completion of this component does not certify the learner as a Contact Investigator. It is an introductory/refresher learning activity designed to support understanding of the CI programme, public health principles, confidentiality, surveillance, partner services, and safe disease intervention practice.

Completion rule: Review the component, complete one final 25-question quiz, score at least 80%, then download a 1 CME / 1 learning-hour certificate.
